## Full Description
Welcome to **FlipLang**, the ultimate language learning survival guide for the post-apocalyptic world!

In a world where communication is as vital as radiation resistance, FlipLang helps you master new languages through an immersive, gamified experience inspired by classic wasteland survival.

### Key Features:
*   **Wasteland Map Progression:** Navigate through different sectors of the wasteland. Each sector is a new lesson. Complete them to unlock more!
*   **Survival Mechanics:** Manage your Hunger and Thirst. Daily practice keeps you healthy. Missing days causes damage!
*   **S.P.E.C.I.A.L. Stats:** Your learning performance affects your stats. High Intelligence helps you learn faster, while Perception helps you in tests.
*   **Perk System:** Earn Bottle Caps and spend them on Perks. Equip up to 3 perks to gain passive bonuses like extra XP or daily Stimpacks.
*   **AI Overseer:** Need help? Ask the AI Overseer for linguistic advice or survival tips.
*   **Holotapes:** Practice your reading skills with authentic wasteland holotapes.
*   **Multi-language Support:** Learn English, Russian, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, and Chinese!

Whether you're a vault dweller or a wasteland wanderer, FlipLang is your best companion for linguistic calibration.

**Prepare for the future. Master a language today!**
