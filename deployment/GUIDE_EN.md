# App Store / Google Play Listing (EN)

## App Name
Fennec Academy: Language Learning

## Short Description
Master new languages in an interactive campus environment with AI mentors and gamified progress.

## Long Description
Welcome to **Fennec Academy**, the premier institution for linguistic mastery in a unique retro-futuristic campus setting.

Fennec Academy blends proven Spaced Repetition Systems (SRS) with an engaging role-playing experience. Whether you're a beginner or a linguistic scholar, our platform adapts to your pace and goals.

**Key Features:**
- **AI Tutors**: Consult with digital Professors and Deans for real-time conversation practice.
- **Interactive Scenarios**: Engage in branching dialogues where your words and choices shape the narrative.
- **Skill Cards**: Customize your learning experience with Perk Cards that boost your cognitive attributes.
- **Educational Video Lessons**: Access a curated library of YouTube lessons integrated directly into your study flow.
- **Full Customization**: Build your student identity with unique accessories and specialized attribute allocation.

Join thousands of scholars and master your target language today. The Fennec Academy campus is waiting for you!
