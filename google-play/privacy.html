Privacy Policy - Fennec Academy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fennec Academy ("Fennec", "we", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, complete lessons, or communicate with our AI assistant (Fennec). This may include:

  • Authentication Data: Email address via Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication)
  • Profile Information: Username, Avatar choice, learning preferences
  • Learning Progress: Completed lessons, vocabulary learned, quiz scores, S.P.E.C.I.A.L. stats, Perks, Vault progress
  • Interaction Data: Messages with AI tutors, conversation history for personalization
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature engagement (for improving the learning experience)

3. How We Use Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Optimize your learning path based on your progress
  • Track progress in RPG-mechanics (S.P.E.C.I.A.L. stats, Perks, Vault rooms)
  • Synchronize your game state across devices via Firebase
  • Provide personalized AI feedback and recommendations
  • Send notifications about learning streaks and achievements (if enabled)

4. Data Security

All data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). Firebase Authentication and Firestore handle authentication and database operations securely.

5. Third-Party Services

Our application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and database management. Data is stored securely in Firebase projects.
  • Google Gemini AI: For AI-powered language tutoring features. User API key (if provided) is stored locally on user's device.
  • YouTube: For video lesson integration. Content is loaded from YouTube's servers and is subject to YouTube's privacy policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7 below).

7. Account and Data Deletion

How to Request Account and Data Deletion

To request deletion of your Fennec account and all associated personal data:

  1. Send an email to photolessonss@gmail.com with subject: "Account Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your Fennec account
  3. We will process your request within 30 days
  4. You will receive confirmation when your data has been deleted

What will be deleted:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • Your learning progress and statistics
  • Your conversation history and AI interaction data
  • Your Vault progress and unlocked items
  • All personal profile information

Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your learning progress cannot be recovered after deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

Fennec is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
