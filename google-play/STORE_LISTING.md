### Short Description (max 80 chars)
Master languages in the wasteland with AI mentors and Vault-Tec technology.

### Full Description
Welcome to the Vault, Citizen!

In a world reclaimed by the wasteland, communication is your greatest survival tool. Fennec presents the ultimate Language Survival Package, designed to turn any survivor into a polyglot master.

### SURVIVE & LEARN
Learning isn't just a hobby—it's survival. Manage your **HP, Hunger, and Thirst** as you progress through lessons. Make a mistake? You'll take damage. Run out of energy? You'll need to scavenge for supplies.

### AI MENTORS
Consult the **Elder** for wasteland wisdom or interface with the **Overseer Terminal** for advanced linguistic diagnostics. Our state-of-the-art AI provides personalized feedback, grammar analysis, and pronunciation tips tailored to your performance.

### BUILD YOUR VAULT
Earn Caps and XP to unlock new Vault rooms. From the **Library** (XP boost) to the **Medbay** (HP regen), each room provides unique perks to aid your learning journey.

### MASTER VOCABULARY
Use our advanced **Spaced Repetition System (SRS)** with flashcards to ensure you never forget a word. Track your progress with the Knowledge Graph and visualize your path to fluency.

### FEATURES:
- **Gamified Learning:** Fallout-inspired HUD and survival mechanics.
- **AI-Powered:** Real-time conversations and personalized learning tips.
- **Vault Building:** Customize your learning environment with functional rooms.
- **Multi-Language Support:** Learn English, Russian, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Chinese, and Serbian.
- **Offline Mode:** Keep learning even when the network goes dark.
