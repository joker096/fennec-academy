## Our Key Advantages

### 1. The S.P.E.C.I.A.L. System
While other apps just track XP, Fennec tracks your **Attributes**:
- **S**trength (Persistence)
- **P**erception (Accuracy in listening)
- **E**ndurance (Lesson length / Stamina)
- **C**harisma (Communication / AI Chat)
- **I**ntelligence (Grammar & Vocabulary retention)
- **A**gility (Speed of response)
- **L**uck (Critical rewards & Item drops)

### 2. Perk Cards
Unlocking perks like "Concentrated Fire" (removes incorrect options) or "Lead Belly" (reduces hunger/thirst decay) adds a layer of strategy that keeps users coming back for more than just a streak.

### 3. Hyper-Adaptive UI
Our UI adapts to your progress. As you master more words, the "Vault Status" improves, and more sections of the "Wasteland Map" become available for exploration.

### 4. Deep AI Personalization
Using Gemini Flash models, we provide:
- **Instant Explanations**: "Why was I wrong?" answered with linguistic context.
- **Visual Aids**: AI-generated imagery for difficult concepts.
- **Pronunciation Feedback**: Real-time analysis of your speech.
