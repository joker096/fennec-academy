## Full Description (English)
**SURVIVE THE WASTELAND. MASTER THE LANGUAGE.**

Step into a post-apocalyptic world where your only hope for survival is your ability to communicate. Lingua AI: Wasteland Survivor is not just a language learning app; it's an immersive RPG experience that turns vocabulary drills into life-or-death decisions.

**FEATURES:**

*   **RPG PROGRESSION:** Build your S.P.E.C.I.A.L. stats (Strength, Perception, Endurance, Charisma, Intelligence, Agility, Luck) to unlock unique perks and bonuses.
*   **INTERACTIVE STORIES:** Navigate branching dialogues and make choices that impact your survival. Every conversation is a lesson.
*   **AI TUTOR (FENNEC):** Your loyal companion Fennec is powered by advanced AI to provide real-time hints, grammar explanations, and pronunciation feedback.
*   **SPACED REPETITION (SRS):** A refined algorithm that adapts to your performance, fatigue, and even the time of day.
*   **OFFLINE MODE:** Practice your flashcards and lessons even when you're deep in the bunkers without a connection.
*   **GAMIFIED LEARNING:** Earn XP, collect Caps, and unlock achievements as you climb the global leaderboard.
*   **MULTIPLE LANGUAGES:** Learn Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Russian, and more!

**Why Lingua AI?**
Traditional apps are boring. Lingua AI makes learning addictive by wrapping it in a compelling narrative. Whether you're scavenging for supplies or negotiating with mutants, you're building real-world language skills.

Join the survivors. Download now and start your journey through the linguistic wasteland!
