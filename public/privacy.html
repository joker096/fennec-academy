Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fennec Academy. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, complete lessons, or communicate with our AI assistant (Fennec). This may include:

  • Profile information (Username, Avatar choice)
  • Learning progress and statistics
  • Interaction data with our AI modules

3. How We Use Data

We use the data to optimize your learning path, track progress in RPG-mechanics (S.P.E.C.I.A.L. stats, Perks), and synchronize your game state across devices via Firebase.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application uses Google Firebase for authentication and database management. Your data is stored securely in Firebase projects.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fennecacademy.example.com
